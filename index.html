Marisa Isabell Keller

PhD | MSCA Postdoc in human-microbiome symbiosis

I am a computational biologist and postdoctoral researcher in the group of Torben Hansen at the Novo Nordisk Foundation Center for Basic Metabolic Research (CBMR), Faculty of Health and Medical Sciences, University of Copenhagen.

My research focuses on human-microbiome interactions, leveraging computational biology, metagenomics, and multi-omics approaches to understand the role of the microbiome in health and disease.
